FAQ

Quick answers so you don’t pass “Go” confused.

Who can attend?

Students of any major/skill level. Beginners welcome — we’ll have mentors and workshops.

Do I need a team?

No. You can build solo or form teams of up to 4 during kickoff.

What should I bring?

Laptop, charger, headphones, and a willingness to ship. We’ll handle snacks and vibes.

How does judging work?

Impact, execution, polish, and clarity of pitch. Bonus for real users or measurable results.
